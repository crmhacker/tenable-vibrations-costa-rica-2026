From Army Grit to Nervous-System Mastery

Dan’s story of burnout, breakthrough, and building Tenable Vibrations

Chapter 1: The Army

Dan started in the U.S. Army, where discipline and resilience were the only options. Early mornings, endless drills, and the pressure of service forged toughness — but also planted the seeds of tension he didn’t yet know how to release.

Chapter 2: The Corporate Grind

After the military, Dan transitioned into corporate sales. He climbed fast, breaking quotas and earning recognition. On the outside, it looked like success. But inside, he was running on fumes — restless sleep, high stress, and a nervous system constantly stuck in overdrive.

“I could close deals and hit numbers, but I couldn’t quiet my mind or find balance.”

Chapter 3: The Breaking Point

The long hours and unrelenting pace caught up. Dan’s health and energy started slipping, and he knew he couldn’t keep going the same way. That’s when he turned inward — searching for practices that could reset not just his body, but his entire way of being.

Chapter 4: The Shift

Breathwork, cold immersion, and nervous-system training weren’t quick fixes — but they were transformative. For the first time since the Army, Dan felt truly calm. His sleep deepened, his energy stabilized, and his focus sharpened in ways coffee and grind culture never delivered.

“I didn’t just feel less stressed — I felt like myself again.”

Chapter 5: The Mission

Today, Dan brings that same intensity and drive to helping others reclaim balance and clarity. Through Tenable Vibrations, he guides high-performers to reset their nervous systems, expand resilience, and reconnect with purpose — tools that saved him, now shared openly.

Be part of the journey

In January 2026, Dan will lead an immersive retreat in Costa Rica — the culmination of his own transformation. For founders, leaders, and creators who want to reset and recharge, this is the opportunity.
