Founder Story

From Army grit to boardroom grind — to nervous-system mastery.

Dan’s real arc: discipline forged in service, success built in sales, and a hard-won return to health that became Tenable Vibrations.

Chapter 1 — The Army

Reveille before sunrise. Miles under a loaded pack. Decisions that mattered. The Army gave Dan an unshakeable engine — focus, standards, resilience. It also hardwired a “never switch off” mode he carried into the rest of life.

“I could outwork anyone. I just didn’t know how to come down.”

Chapter 2 — The Corporate Climb

Sales came naturally: clear targets, clean scoreboards, big wins. Dan climbed fast — President’s Club, stretch goals, bigger accounts. On paper, everything soared. Inside, sleep thinned, shoulders crept to his ears, and the motor never idled.

“I was winning deals…and losing the quiet in my head.”

Chapter 3 — The Crash You Don’t See

It didn’t look like collapse. It looked like another promotion with a body that whispered no. Headaches. Shallow breaths. Sundays that felt like Mondays. Dan didn’t want out — he wanted control back.

Chapter 4 — The Shift

Breathwork. Cold immersion. Nervous-system training. Not hacks — practices. The first win was simple: deeper sleep. Then steadier mornings. Fewer spikes, faster recoveries. Meetings felt cleaner. Work felt lighter. Life felt…his.

“I didn’t just feel less stressed — I felt like myself again.”

Chapter 5 — Tenable Vibrations

Dan turned what saved him into a mission. Tools that fit real lives: founders, leaders, athletes — people with stakes. No fluff, no extremes. Just protocols that build capacity and peace at the same time.

Breathwork • Cold • Mobility • Integration • Community
